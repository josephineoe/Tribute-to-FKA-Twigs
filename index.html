Tahliah Debrett Barnett was born on the 17th of January 1988 in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire. She waa raised mainly by her birth-mother and step-father (who apparently was a "jazz-fanatic") Born to be a star, she undertook opera and ballet lessons and took part in several at her secondary schools productions. At age 16, Barnett started making music in youth clubs. At 17, she moved to South London to pursue a career as a dancer, where she also attended the BRIT School. After changing her focus from dance to music, she transferred from the BRIT School to Croydon College to pursue an education in fine arts. She worked as a backup dancer in music videos by artists such as Kylie Minogue, Plan B, Ed Sheeran, Taio Cruz, Dionne Bromfield, Jessie J, and Wretch 32.
